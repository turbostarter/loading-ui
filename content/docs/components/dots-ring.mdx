## Customization

`DotsRing` is more configurable than most of the simpler spinners in the set. Since the component exposes props for dot count, pulse timing, dot size, and ring radius, it is a good fit when you want to tune the motion itself instead of only swapping utility classes around it.

### Size

Use `size-*` utilities to control the overall footprint of the ring in compact and larger layouts.

<ComponentPreview name="dots-ring-size" />

### Dot Count

Increase or decrease the number of dots to make the motion feel denser or more minimal.

<ComponentPreview name="dots-ring-dots" />

### Duration

The `duration` prop adjusts how quickly the pulse travels around the ring.

<ComponentPreview name="dots-ring-duration" />

### Dot Scale

Use `dotScale` to change the size of each dot relative to the overall ring.

<ComponentPreview name="dots-ring-dot-scale" />

### Radius Scale

The `radiusScale` prop controls how far the dots sit from the center, which changes how tight or airy the ring feels.

<ComponentPreview name="dots-ring-radius-scale" />
